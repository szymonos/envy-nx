# Ideas

Build a validation skill based on the [AI-Native Startup Playbook](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/69fe2a55b93bb0732b1fe33c_The-Founders-Playbook-05062026_v3%20(1).pdf)

- Defining and pressure-testing the problem hypothesis
- Market research and mapping the competitive landscape
- Plan and design customer discovery
- Customer outreach and scheduling
- Design your final solution concept

Architecture validation

- create requirements that define your solution
- spawn a subagent to create an architecture
- validate the architecture with the actuaal architecture and:
  - confirm the flaws in the current architecture and craete a correcting document to fix it, or
  - redefine requirements, if the proposed architecture didn't fit the case
  - create a loop of iterations to fine tune the architecture.
